A View Through Them

Center / About The Vision

AVTT Iniaitive

This is their story, how they see it.

A View Through Them (AVTT) stands to center the sounds often underrepresented, misrepresented or, all together, not present in mainstream media, academia and classical canons. The expression of the Indigenous, African, and most broadly people of color were, and still are, to a certain extent, historically cast aside. How can there be art without refrence to the situation of Earth's copper skinned population? While this effort strives to honor and protect the diverse nature of humankind's record, it also plays as a reminder of the values and emotions we share as a people. The AVTT Initiative is a contribution: consciously coloring in the master narrative, the collective memory, one voice at a time.

"We need to sing with all the voices of the mountain. We need to paint with all the colors of the wind."

KeyAndre and Company

About The Group's mission

KeyAndre & Company is a lifestyle brand that encompasses literature, media, and education, creating content with youth in mind. Established in 2016 by Kordell K. Hammond, the group is on a mission to encourage self-awareness, self-discipline and self-care through the art of design, rhetoric and rehabilitation.
