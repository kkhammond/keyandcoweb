What we're reading ...

As a kid I only really enjoyed storytime when being read to by Grandma CC, or a teacher at school. And reading by myself, on my own time? You could forget about it ... Since graduating from SUNY Fredonia, I’ve found reading literary texts with a focus on relational dynamics, to impact and continuously re-define the social, structural, and metaphorical relationships in my own life.

Today, as an instructional designer who works 1:1 in many different settings, it’s smart I diversify my bookshelf. In addition to academic scholarship which (directly) addresses my research, my commitment to reading beyond necessity has informed my practice as a modern day UX Writer.

Below you can find what books I’ve found most interesting in moments of leisure. And I’m always ready for recommendations so please feel free to reach out, I’d love to dance!
