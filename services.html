About Our Services

KeyAndre Services™
As a public practice offering private support, we also coordinate outreach efforts to promote humanities-related educational projects and need-based enrichment services. KeyAndre Services include but are not limited to: analytical consultation for the design and development of holistic curricula, learning strategies, educational policies, and courseware standards. We work with our clients as we do with our practitioners: Aware we are each the learner and educator always, and in all ways.

Bookings and Consultation
If you or your education system are intrested in leanring more about our services, we welcome you to schedule a complimentary 20-30 minute consultation session. Depending on our practioner's availability and pending COVID saftey measures, this consultation can be set over the phone, via video, or in-person.
