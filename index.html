Our Principles

In autumn of 2017, ten undergraduate students, myself included, set out to produce an audio-documentary. Instead, we had envisioned something more far-reaching. Founded on a mission to encourage self-awareness, self-discipline and self-care, through the art of design, rhetoric and rehabilitation, the group would later call themselves the KeyAndre & Company. This extracurricular activity turned "lifestyle brand" provides us the room to imagine, shape and share humanities-related educational projects.

KeyAndre & Company (d.b.a KeyAndre) acts as a research center and data distribution company. We plan, design, and develop teaching and learning products (including web-based aids, audio-articles and audio-books) with youth in mind. We specialize in recording, codifying, and providing access to oral traditions from the Indigenous and African diaspora.
